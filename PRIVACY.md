# Privacy Policy - EnorEtt Chrome Extension

**Last Updated:** October 2025

## OVERVIEW

EnorEtt respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## DATA COLLECTION

EnorEtt does NOT collect:
- Personal information
- Browsing history
- Search queries
- Usage statistics
- Any other data

## LOCAL STORAGE

EnorEtt stores the following data LOCALLY on your device only:
- Your word lookup history (optional, can be cleared)
- Favorite words (optional)
- Extension settings/preferences

This data NEVER leaves your device.

## PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED

- **"storage"** - To save your settings and history locally
- **"contextMenus"** - To add right-click functionality
- **No website access** - We cannot see what websites you visit

## THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

EnorEtt does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## OPTIONAL API ACCESS (Pro Users)

Pro users who choose to use our API may have their word lookups processed by our servers. This data is:
- Not linked to your identity
- Not stored longer than necessary for processing
- Not shared with third parties
- Encrypted in transit

## CHANGES TO POLICY

We will notify users of any material changes to this privacy policy via extension updates.

## CONTACT

Questions about privacy: privacy@enorett.se
